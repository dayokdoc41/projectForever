BEST LOVE STORY

Once upon a time, there was a boy who by chance run across a girl.

They were on the same company as co-employees, then later became friends.

Days, weeks, years have passed and the friendship is getting strong and deep.

The boy secretly fall in love with the girl, but cannot profess his feelings towards the girl.

He is afraid that the friendship might end if he confess how he feels.

Also, the girl is in a long term relationship with someone else.

More years have passed and the boy decided to look for some else.

Not knowing that the girl feels the seem way as he do.

By good fortune, the boy re-consider his decision.

He then decided to tell the girl everything and the girl accepted the boy.
